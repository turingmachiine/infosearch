List of NFL quarterbacks with 5,000 passing yards in a season

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Passing for 5,000 yards in a single regular season is a rare thing in the National Football League (NFL). Nine quarterbacks have done it. Drew Brees has the most seasons with 5,000 passing yards at 5.[1]

NFL players with 5,000 passing yards in a season[change | change source]

Quarterback Team Yards Season Notes
Dan Marino* Miami Dolphins 5,084 1984 1st in NFL history, youngest to achieve 5,000 yards,[2]
NFL MVP
Drew Brees New Orleans Saints 5,069 2008 Offensive Player of the Year[3]
Drew Brees (2) New Orleans Saints 5,476 2011 Offensive Player of the Year
Tom Brady New England Patriots 5,235 [4]
Matthew Stafford Detroit Lions 5,038 Comeback Player of the Year[5]
Drew Brees (3) New Orleans Saints 5,177 2012 2nd consecutive
Peyton Manning* Denver Broncos 5,477 2013 NFL record,[6] NFL MVP
Drew Brees (4) New Orleans Saints 5,162 3rd consecutive
Drew Brees (5) New Orleans Saints 5,208 2016
Ben Roethlisberger Pittsburgh Steelers 5,129 2018 [7]
Patrick Mahomes* Kansas City Chiefs 5,097 NFL MVP, first African-American quarterback to pass for 5,000 yards
Jameis Winston Tampa Bay Buccaneers 5,109 2019 Led NFL in interceptions (30)[8]
Tom Brady (2) Tampa Bay Buccaneers 5,316 2021 Oldest to achieve 5,000 yards
Justin Herbert Los Angeles Chargers 5,014 [9]
